Job Description

Reporting to the Data Manager in the Emory Office of the Registrar, the Data Analyst will support the translation of student record data into dashboards and reports to drive institutional strategy, performance and decision-making. The Data Analyst will support efforts to ensure that data standards are developed and maintained and ensure that the uses of data through reports, queries and BI solutions are accurate.

Additionally the Data Analyst will support business and system re-engineering and architecture development to define future data needs. This role will play an integral part in supporting Emory’s investment in the new Student Data Warehouse, serving as an institutional consultant on matters relating to both the data warehouse and Peoplesoft reporting, by providing expertise to assist users in meeting their needs.

Job Description

Creates and maintains a data dictionary and meta data. Supports efforts to ensure that data standards are developed and maintained. Ensures that the uses of data through reports and queries are accurate.

Supports business and system re-engineering and architecture development to define future data needs. Serves as an organizational consultant on matters relating to databases by providing expertise to assist users in meeting their needs.
